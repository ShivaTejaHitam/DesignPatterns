## 8. Follow-Up Questions Interviewers Love

* How will you **handle concurrency**?
* Where will you use **interfaces vs abstract classes**?
* How do you **extend this design without modifying existing code**?
* How do you **persist state**?
* How do you **handle failures / retries**?
* How do you **write unit tests for this design**?

## What Interviewers Expect (Direct)

* Clear **class diagrams**
* Proper **responsibility separation**
* Use of **SOLID principles**
* Correct **design patterns**
* Thread-safety awareness
* Clean method signatures
